Profile

Who I Am

Molecular biologist, CTO, and photographer based in Uppsala, Sweden. PhD from SLU under Prof. Eugene Savenkov — focused on plant virus biology, RNA silencing, and host-pathogen interactions. Postdoc at SLU Alnarp, where I pioneered spray-induced gene silencing (SIGS) for crop protection against Phytophthora infestans.

Today I'm CTO & Co-Founder of a pre-seed biotech startup, translating a decade of RNA science into commercial impact. I've also mentored 7 master's students and 2 PhD candidates, and taught across 12 courses.
