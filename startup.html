My Role

CTO & Co-Founder

Since March 2025, I have been building a pre-seed biotechnology startup from the ground up — from scientific concept to validated technology to commercial partnerships.

As CTO, I am responsible for the scientific strategy, technology development roadmap, IP protection, and building the technical team. In a pre-seed startup, "CTO" also means chief scientist, lab head, protocol designer, and often the person who fixes the centrifuge.

The technology we are developing — a proprietary post-harvest biological treatment platform — addresses one of the most overlooked sustainability problems in global food and flower supply chains: perishable waste driven by fungal disease.

The Problem We're Solving

Perishable waste is one of the most overlooked sustainability crises in global trade. Fungal disease is the leading driver of post-harvest losses — from farms, through logistics, to retail shelves.

Our proprietary bio-based treatment platform addresses this problem at its molecular source — precisely, sustainably, and without harmful chemical residues.

Journey

From Lab Bench to Startup

The transition from academic researcher to biotech entrepreneur — and what it takes to translate a decade of science into a company.

2015–2024
The Research Foundation
SLU Uppsala and Alnarp

Over nine years of research building the scientific foundation: plant virology, RNA silencing, dsRNA crop protection, biocontrol interactions. 12+ publications. The science that would eventually become a company was being built, paper by paper.

2021
The Breakthrough
Publication in Phytopathology

First published demonstration of spray-induced gene silencing protecting plants from Phytophthora infestans. This result showed that RNA-based crop protection was not just theoretically possible — it worked in the greenhouse. The seed of a company was planted.

Early 2025
The Decision to Build
Stockholm / Uppsala

After completing my postdoc and a brief stint at Thalya, I co-founded my startup with the conviction that the global perishable supply chain had a problem that decade of plant biology research could solve. We started building.

Mar 2025 – Present
Building in Public
Stealth Biotech Startup · Uppsala & Mannheim

From a two-person founding team to a six-member technical team. From a concept to greenhouse-validated technology. From zero to 12 LOIs and active pilots across the US–LATAM rose corridor. The science is working. The commercial traction is real.

Vision

"Science should not stop at the journal page."

The problems that motivated my research — crop losses, supply chain waste, the unsustainable reliance on synthetic chemicals — are not going to be solved by publications alone. They need products. Companies. Commercial deployment at scale.

My mission is to be the scientist who sees the journey through from first experiment to real-world impact — across the full TRL spectrum, from lab to greenhouse to market.
