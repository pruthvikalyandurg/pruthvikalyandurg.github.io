Publications & Research

Research Themes

Areas of Investigation

Plant Pathology
Plant Disease & Protection

From characterising PMTV diversity in Peru to pioneering spray-induced gene silencing against potato late blight — targeting plant diseases at their molecular root.

RNA Biology
RNA Interference & Gene Silencing

Investigating viral suppressors of RNA silencing, dsRNA delivery mechanisms, cross-kingdom sRNA signalling, and Dicer-dependent RNAi in fungal interactions.

Sustainable Agriculture
Biocontrol & Plant Growth

Characterising beneficial microbes — from biocontrol bacteria to mycoparasitic fungi — as alternatives to chemical pesticides and synthetic fertilisers.
