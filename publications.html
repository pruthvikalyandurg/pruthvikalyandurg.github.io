Research themes
Plant pathology

Disease & protection

From characterising virus diversity across continents to demonstrating RNA-based protection against potato late blight, targeting disease at its molecular root.

RNA biology

Interference & silencing

Viral suppressors of RNA silencing, small-RNA delivery, cross-kingdom signalling, and Dicer-dependent RNAi in fungal interactions.

Sustainable agriculture

Biocontrol & growth

Beneficial microbes, from biocontrol bacteria to mycoparasitic fungi, characterised as alternatives to chemical inputs.
