About

Where Molecules Meet Markets

Born in India, trained in Sweden — I have spent over a decade studying the molecular conversations between plants and their pathogens at the Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences (SLU).

My PhD at SLU Uppsala explored how plant viruses hijack cellular machinery for systemic movement. My postdoctoral work at SLU Alnarp pioneered dsRNA spray technologies to protect potatoes from Phytophthora infestans — the pathogen behind the Irish Potato Famine.

Today, as CTO & Co-Founder of a stealth pre-seed biotechnology startup, I am translating that decade of research into scalable, sustainable crop protection solutions.
