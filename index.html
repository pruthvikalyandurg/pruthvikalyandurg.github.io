About

Where Molecules Meet Markets

PhD from SLU Uppsala in plant virology, postdoc at SLU Alnarp pioneering RNA-based crop protection. Grew up watching farmers in Anantapur — still chasing the same problem, just closer to solving it.

Now CTO & Co-Founder of a pre-seed biotech startup, translating a decade of research into real supply chain impact.
